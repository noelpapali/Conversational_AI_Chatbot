The Bursar’s Office is responsible for theassessment, billing and collection of tuition and feesat The University of Texas at Dallas.
Our mission is to manage all financial transactions involving students, faculty and staff withefficiency and accuracy, while consistently deliveringrespectful and exceptional customer service.
We hope the information provided on this website helps you better understand the functions of the Bursar’s Office and supports students in meeting their financial responsibilities at UT Dallas.
Please note:The Bursar’s Office operatesvirtuallyand isnot available for in-person transactions.
We encourage you to take advantage of ouronline servicesto make payments and access important information. If you need assistance, we’re here to help.
Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to assisting you!

Lists:
- Monday, July 6, 2026– The payment deadline for students enrolled in only the 2nd5-week session classes.
- Wednesday, July 29, 2026 –This deadline applies to all students enrolled in the regular and 1st8 weeks sessions by July 22, 2026, and arenotidentified as a financial aid or scholarship recipient. This does not include new incoming students for the fall 2026 term.
- Thursday, August 20, 2026– This deadline applies to all students enrolled in the regular and 1st8 weeks sessions beginning July 23, 2026, and arenotidentified as a financial aid or scholarship recipient. This does not include new incoming students for the fall 2026 term.
- Thursday, September 3, 2026– This deadline applies to all new incoming students, as well as students identified as a financial aid or a scholarship recipient. This extension of time is only to allow more time for the aid or scholarships to be posted and has no bearing on the refund calendar for dropping/withdrawing from classes.  All students must be either paid in full or enrolled in a payment plan by this date.
- Thursday, September 3, 2026– All housing, meal plan and insurance charges are due in full, or an installment plan must be in effect by this date.
